IceBreaker Game
Rules: Icebreaker is a game that consists of twoplayers who make 
two actions in one turn;

The first action is to move their game piece in any legal adjacent square, 

The second action is they can break the 'ice' anywhere on the board.
once those two actions are done the turns switch and it is now player 2's turn. 

The game ends when one player is surrounded by tiles of 
broken ice and can no longer make a legal move.
